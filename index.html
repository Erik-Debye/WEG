Reliable

When you use WEG, you are guaranteed to get to your destination as fast as possible.


WEG uses state-of-the-art algorithms developed from the millions of rides WEG has completed to develop the most quickest and most efficient routes.

02/03

Eco-Friendly

Climate change affects all of us, but WEG is the first 100% Electric Rideshare company.


This means that when you use WEG, you can rest easy knowing you did your part in reducing emissions by traveling efficiently.


WEG also donates 10% of each fare to the Great Green Wall initiative, a project restoring 100 million hectres of land spanning 11 African countries.

Who We Are

WEG started from a collective of environmentalists, visionaries, and economists who seek to disrupt the current rideshare market with a safer, more reliable, and more environmentally concious transit option.

Everyone at WEG strives to make transit options more affordable, more efficent, more depandable, and more green for everyone.

Using advanced ride analysis, customer metrics, and other data, we hope to use complex analysis to see where we can make a substantial difference in transit solutions around the world and in your neighborhood.
