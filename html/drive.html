Looking for work?
Join WEG as a driver

With an array of benefits, including same-day payment, WEG invites all qualified to join us in disrupting the transit market.

Benefits

Get Paid Same Day

Become a WEG driver and enjoy the convenience of same-day paychecks for your hard work.

With our innovative payment system, you can access your earnings as soon as you complete a ride, without having to wait for the end of the week or month.

Whether you want to make extra cash on the side or work full-time, we offer competitive rates and a hassle-free experience that puts you in control.

Work when you want

With the flexibility to work as much or as little as you want, you can easily balance work with other commitments and responsibilities.

Whether you're a stay-at-home parent, a student, or just looking for a side hustle, our platform allows you to earn money on your own terms.

Plus, with no minimum time commitment or fixed schedules, you have the flexibility to take time off whenever you need it.

Join a community

Whether you're a seasoned driver or new to the platform, we're here to support you every step of the way.

And when it's time to unwind, we offer weekly driver gatherings at a local bar where you can connect with other drivers, share your experiences, and relax in a casual and fun atmosphere.

Join us and become a part of a community that celebrates diversity and promotes mutual respect and support among all its members.

No EV? No Problem.

We're proud to offer our electric car rental program, which allows qualified, safe drivers to rent an electric vehicle for as low as $120 per week.

With our rental program, you can enjoy the benefits of driving an electric car, including lower fuel costs, reduced emissions, and a smoother, quieter ride.

Our rental cars are also equipped with the latest technology and safety features to ensure your comfort and peace of mind while driving.

Requirements

You need all the below to qualify as a WEG Driver

  • Be 25+ years of age

  • Pass an in-depth background check

  • Be eligible to work within the USA

  • Hold a Driver's License

  • Proof of residency within your state

  • Own (or rent) an electric vehicle
