WEG understands safety is paramount.

That's why WEG commits to in-depth background checks of every driver, and offers passengers the option to undergo their own background check for a lifetime discount.

Riders

  • WEG uses advanced, real-time GPS technology to ensure we can track every ride from start to destination.

  • Our app offers in-app emergency assistence, location sharing, and detailed driver and vehicle profiles.

  • Drivers are trained to verify their passengers by scanning your in-app bar code.

  • A 24/7 support team is available to handle every scenario as it unfolds.

  • Passengers have an opportunity to pay $30 once to get verified and unlock a lifetime 5% discount.

Drivers

  • WEG understands that drivers can face difficult challenges. Our separate Driver support team is available 24/7.

  • Along with real-time tracking, WEG offers in-app navigation for both you and your passenger.

  • In-app barcode scanning means you'll never pick up the wrong person again.

  • Drivers can choose to switch between only offering rides to verified passengers at any time.

  • When crises occur, you can count on your regional WEG team to support you.
