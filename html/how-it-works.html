How can you call a WEG Ride?

Easy!

First, download the WEG app on your smart phone.

Next, create your account.

Go ahead and click "Get a Ride"

Fill out your trip details and press confirm

Now it's our job to connect you to nearby drivers

Those drivers see your destination, mileage, and duration of your ride.

Within 4 minutes, a driver will be headed on their way to pick you up!

Once you enter the car and scan your barcode, you're set!

Enjoy the ride!
